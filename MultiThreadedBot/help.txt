Quick guide to VideoSubtitler_bot.
This bot is designed to search and create subtitles for video from YouTube.
You can give him a link to the video and if the subtitles are found on server you immediately receive subtitles. Else if automatic subtitles are found, they will be downloaded and sent to you by several messages. Otherwise, audio will be downloaded and processed and sent to you in small messages.
You can use the optional parameter "en" (or "eng", "EN", "english") to recognize speech in english language and optional parameter "no_sub" to ignore youtube subtitles. The order of the parameters is important.
Example: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d8BWU7vxGjk eng no_sub
It is all information you should know about this bot. Good using!
